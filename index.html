The Best

Through time, there have been some greats, from Pele to Maradona to Lionel Messi, but none beat the one, the only, Kanye West. But no one seems to realize that there are more than a few great players. Even today, we have Messi, Ronaldo, Neymar, Hazard and the list can keep going. The point is, there are a lot of good players, you just have to be paying attention. So let's go into a few that have been doing phenominal this season.

Premier League

    Eden Hazard
  • The Belgian beast from Chelsea known as Eden Hazard has been incredible this season. Just this month, he has scored 5 goals and assisted 3, enough to surely lock up the player of the month.
    • Harry Kane
  • Arguably one of the best strikers in the world, Harry Kane continues to do what he has always done, score goals. Being directly involved in over half of Spurs’ goals this season, Harry Kane shows why he is one of the best in the Premier League.
    • N’ngolo Kante
  • Coming back from the French national team in which he won the world cup this summer, N’golo Kante deserves a spot on this list. Although not the goalscoring kind, he is constantly all over the pitch to defend and control the Chelsea Squad.

    La Liga Santander

      Lionel Messi
    • In the talks as the one who is possibly the best of all time, Lionel Messi does not ever fail to impress, including this season. Currently sitting at 3rd in the top scorer list and 1st in assists, Leo Messi is a force to be reckoned with as he looks to dominate for another year.
      • Christhian Stuani
    • Sitting at the top scorer of La Liga right now, Christhian Stuani is having a great year. He leads the Girona side with 8 goals this season. With this, he shows no sign of slowing down any time soon.
      • Luka Modric
    • Coming off of his incredible world cup with Croatia, Luka Modric is coming to show why he is one of the best in the world. Controlling Real Madrid’s midfield, he has already won the Fifa Best award for this year and World Cup Player of the Tournament.

    Rest of World

      Cristiano Ronaldo
    • The current king of the Champions League, Cristiano Ronaldo, shocked the world when he left Real Madrid for Juventus. Now, he looks to continue his goalscoring form with the Italian side. With this, coming his talents as the top scorer in Champions League history.
      • Neymar Jr
    • After a disappointing year in the Champions League, going out to Real Madrid in the round of 16, Neymar and PSG look to bounce back. Neymar currently sits at the top of the French League scorer standings and looks to continue his run.
